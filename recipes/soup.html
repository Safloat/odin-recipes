Chicken Corn Soup

This soup is your quick winter fix. It includes a combination of chicken broth and corn for some warm goodness. If youâ€™re feeling slightly under the weather, we can assure you this recipe will make you feel nourished, refreshed and healthy for the cold season.

Ingredients

  1. Creamed Corn
  2. Stock
  3. Salt, Pepper and Ginger
  4. Soy Sauce
  5. Vinegar
  6. Egg
  7. Cornflour
  8. Shredded Chicken

Method

  1. Add all Ingredients to the stock.
  2. Bring to a boil, and cook for a couple of minutes
  3. Add cornflour slurry to thicken the soup.
  4. Add whisked egg slowly to the soup.
  5. Enjoy your freshly prepared soup!
